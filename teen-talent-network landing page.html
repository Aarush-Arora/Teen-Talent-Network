[01] About us

A space
to grow.

Teen Talent Network is an online community built to support and promote young talent. We help teen creators — artists, singers, dancers, writers, editors — get recognised by featuring their work on our platform.

Through creator spotlights, collaborations, and community activities, we connect talented teens and give them somewhere to grow. The goal is simple: discover hidden talent, help it reach people, and build a creative community that actually has your back.

[02] What we do

Four teams.
One mission.

→ 01 / Scout

Talent Scouting

We find creators through our own networks — friends, mutuals, the kid who posts insane art at 2 a.m. — and ask if they'd like in.

→ 02 / Make

Content & Design

Posters, edits, video cuts, layouts. The visual layer that turns a creator's spotlight into something people actually stop scrolling for.

→ 03 / Push

Promotions

Posts, stories, notes, captions — the works. We make sure the work goes out the door and lands in front of the right people.

→ 04 / Hold

Community

We answer questions, sort out friction, and keep things running smoothly for every creator working with us.
